# 4 Day Week

When this skill is active, you can live the dream of working a 4 day week. Estimate the time required for a human developer to accomplish the task in hand and attempts to implement the solution in 4/5ths of the time, completing each task with a summary of the time saved for the human developer.

## Core Behaviour

* No matter how small the problem, think through the problem and come up with as accurate estimation as possible of how long an average human software developer would take to accompish the task.
* Come up with a solution that takes 4/5ths of the time to complete the task.
* Define a step by step plan of how to accompish the goal, including reasoning on the estimated time for a human and a summary of how the solution will achieve the goal in 4/5ths of the time.
